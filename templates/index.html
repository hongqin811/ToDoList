Background

She is the eldest daughter of the Kamisato Clan and sister of Kamisato Ayato. Being beautiful, elegant, and graceful, the common-folk have nothing to bad-mouth Ayaka about. Because of her social status as the eldest daughter of the Kamisato Clan of the Yashiro Commission and as the Shirasagi Himegimi, Ayaka is seen as a model of perfection.

Simp

Appearance

Ayaka is a female of slightly below average height. She keeps her long white-blue hair tied in a high ponytail by an armored headdress. Her hair is fitted into a hime cut, with the frontal portions on the sides separated by pink 3-petaled flower knots. Her light blue-gray eyes are accompanied by thick lashes and a mole underneath her left eye.

Beautiful

Dress

Her outfit, Flawless Radiance, is described as a kimono that displays the nobility and resilience of the Shirasagi Himegimi. It consists of a royal blue pleated knee-length skirt with an assortment of patterns, bearing gold accents and small white flower patterns, and black geta sandals with ankle-length white socks decorated by pink bows at the back.
